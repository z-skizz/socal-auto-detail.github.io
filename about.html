About Us

Careful work,
start to finish.

SoCal Luxury Detailing is a mobile detailing service built around one idea: your vehicle deserves the same hand-finished care as a showroom car — without the shop drop-off or the wait.

Why Mobile

We come to you.

Dropping a car off at a shop and losing an afternoon in a waiting room isn't how detailing should work. We bring the water, power, and equipment directly to your driveway, office lot, or curb — so the only thing you have to do is hand over the keys.

Every job, whether it's a full interior refresh or a complete exterior detail, is worked by hand and checked panel by panel before we call it finished. No shortcuts, no upsells you didn't ask for — just careful, consistent work across Orange County and the Inland Empire.
